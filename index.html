My name is Tobechukwu Victor Aki. I'm a fullstack web developer in the making, a game developer, and a lifelong learner.

My Story

My journey in Web development begins way back in Junior Secondary after I finished junior WAEC. I went to an academy where I learnt how to make webpages using HTML. It was from there that I developed an interest in web development, until I lost the book where I write my code, and my interest for web development went with it. My interest for it came back during my 2nd year in university after my friend wanted me to join him on a project he was working on. I realized later that there was more to web development than just HTML. So I relearnt HTML and proceeded to learn CSS. But after my day got so busy (school and my game dev) and because web dev was not my priority at that time, I decided to leave it. During my fourth year, when I was supposed to go for my IT. I had no idea what I wanted to learn, seeing as my school won't regard game development as something that can solve a problem. I decided to learn software development as games are technically a form of software. I then chose to go to Tenece Professional Services where I am currently working as a backend development intern. It was from there that I was introduced to Genesys Upskill and here I am now. My goal is to perfect frontend development through learnable then I can become a certified fullstack web developer.

Fun Fact: Web development is somewhat like software development as it also comprises as frontend and backend development

Reason for joining web developmet... again

Now I know some of you all maybe asking; After the number of times I dived into web development, why did I bother coming back? Well I would say because my mindset has changed since then. I rejoined web development because I want something to add to my portfolio. I also never want anything I find myself doing or learning to ever go to waste and I want to be the very best I can be.
